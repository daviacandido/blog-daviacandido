I am a DevOps & Infrastructure professional with 7 years of experience building and scaling reliable systems. My career is rooted in a solid 6-year foundation managing large-scale mission-critical environments—handling up to 4,000 servers and 65,000 monitored items—which evolved into a dedicated focus on DevOps and Site Reliability Engineering over the past year.

I specialize in bridging the gap between on-premise (bare metal/virtualization) and cloud-native SaaS architectures. My expertise includes designing and orchestrating environments on AWS (EC2, EKS) and Azure (AKS), with a strong focus on node management, security, and advanced scaling.

Driven by a "reliability-first" mindset, I am passionate about eliminating manual toil through automation and ensuring high availability for complex infrastructures.
